What does Kind Food do?
Kind Food is a platform that connects partnered restaurants and shops with customers to reduce food waste. We offer discounted meals through our Foodie Bag program, allowing you to enjoy delicious food while contributing to a sustainable cause.

What is Foodie Bag?
Foodie Bag is a concept where partnered restaurants and shops pack leftover food in random assortments for customers to purchase at a discounted price. It's an exciting way to discover new flavors and help reduce food waste.

Do I need to register to buy Kind Food?
Yes, registration is required to purchase Kind Food. By creating an account, you can access the full features of our platform, including ordering Foodie Bags and enjoying the benefits of our service.

How to cancel my booking?
To cancel your booking, simply log into your account and navigate to the "My Bookings" section. Find the specific booking you wish to cancel and follow the provided instructions to complete the cancellation process. Please note any applicable cancellation policies or deadlines that may apply.

Why should I join Kind Food?
Joining Kind Food offers several benefits. By becoming a partner, you can reduce food waste, reach a wider customer base, and contribute to a sustainable cause. Additionally, partnering with Kind Food can increase your visibility and attract socially conscious customers.

I cannot receive order notifications. What should I do?
If you are not receiving order notifications, please ensure that your contact information, especially your email address and phone number, is correctly entered in your account settings. Double-check your spam or junk folders as well. If the issue persists, please reach out to our support team for assistance.

What should I do if customers do not pick up their order?
If customers do not pick up their order within the designated timeframe, kindly wait for the specified duration. If they still haven't collected their order, you may contact our support team for guidance on how to proceed. They will provide you with the necessary instructions to handle such situations.

There is an error in the back office. How can I ask for help?
If you encounter an error or technical issue in the back office, please reach out to our support team immediately. You can contact them through the dedicated support channel or submit a support ticket. Provide detailed information about the error you're experiencing, and our team will assist you in resolving the issue.
